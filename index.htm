What We Do

About Us

We are Raccoon Rescue and our missions is to offer sanctuary and care to our food bandit friends! Mainly operating out of Tucson, Arizona, we have saved over 20 raccoons from the Western United States.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread awareness of these majestic creatures and offer to rehome raccoons that cannot get along with their human neighbors.
